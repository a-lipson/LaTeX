\begin{problem}
Show that $\Q[x]/(x^2)$ is not a field.
\end{problem}
\begin{proof}
	Since $x^2$ is reducible in $\Q[x]$, factoring as $x\cdot x$, then $x$ is a zero divisor in  $\Q[x]/(x^2)$, and hence $\Q[x]/(x^2)$ is not a field.
\end{proof}
